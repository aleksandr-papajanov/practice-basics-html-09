Surströmming Sandwich

(Surströmmingsklämma)

  • Under 45 minutes
  • 7 ingredients
  • Medium difficulty

Curious about how a real Swedish Surströmming sandwich tastes? Try our recipe featuring traditional toppings like potatoes, fermented herring, red onion, butter, and soft flatbread. It's simple and truly a delicacy!

Ingredients

Serves: 4 portions

  • 1 kg almond or new potatoes
  • 1 can fermented herring (approx. 475 g)
  • 2 red onions
  • 1/2 dl finely chopped chives
  • Butter
  • Soft flatbread
  • Sour cream

Instructions

  1. Wash and boil the potatoes in lightly salted water until soft.
  2. Drain and clean the fermented herring. Peel and finely chop the onions. Slice the potatoes.
  3. Spread butter on the flatbread, add sliced potatoes, onions, chives, and fermented herring. Top with a spoonful of sour cream and roll into wraps.
